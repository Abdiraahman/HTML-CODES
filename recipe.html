Fried rice recipe

The well known fried rice is prepared from oil, vegetables,rice and spices.

Ingredients

The ingredients together with their quantities is in the table below.

Item Quantity
onion 1
rice 1/2kg
tomatoes 2
potatoes 2
souces i.e curry powder,pepper,
royco cubes ,garlic and oil

Procedure

Follow the procedure below to prepare a good meal of fried rice that is meant for three people.

  • Heat a teaspoon of oil in the pot.
  • Dice the onions,tomatoes and potatoes into small cubes and mince the garlic.
  • Add the diced onions and potatoes and the garlic into the pot together
    with curry powder, royco cubes and pepper and saute them till they look golden and softened.
  • Wash the 1/2kg rice till clean. Add it into the pot and stir till the rice is heated through and combined with ingredients.
  • Add two cups of water, stir and place the lid for 5 minutes.
  • Turn of the heat and let the rice sit and steam.
  • It is ready to be served.
